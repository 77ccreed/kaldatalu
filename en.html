Accommodation
Accommodation

We offer accommodation on various levels: from very basic and simple to more comfortable and luxurious. So please make up your mind what you are looking for and what suits you best.

If you book a private room shared showers are included. All other accommodation will have to pay a small fee.

Unfortunately we dont serve any meals. For guests in private rooms we just serve a substantial breakfast as a cold board, in all shared rooms coffee and tea making possibilities are provided. A fridge is provided as well.

Accommodation

Camping site

The camping site has got sufficient room for up to 100 persons. Camp fires may be lit; the sandy ground is very convenient in heavy showers - it will be dry again soon after the rain. In the centre of the site there are three flag poles and a wooden tent in Laponian style with a fireplace. Seven persons can sleep there (or 20 can gather round the open fire).

Of interest to scouts and other youth groups: we rent out a marquee for about 40 persons.

Folding seats and tables for about 40 persons are available as well. For larger groups we have a roofed, but open kitchen place with a stove, gas cookers and pots and pans for bigger quantities of food.

Foodstuff can be stored in a cellar and in metall boxes. In the wood shed there is sufficient wood for cooking and campfires.

For the lovers of barbecues we supply gas and charcoal grills of various sizes (for grill sausages as well as for lambs).

Our electric washing machine may be used at a small fee.

Mobile homes and caravans

For mobile homes and caravans there are four sites in the shade of big trees. Electricity is supplied. There are plenty of parking lots, too.

Water and gas can be supplied, toilets can be emptied.

Camping houses

Simple accomodation is supplied in two radiator-heated camping houses with a double bed and bunk bed each. There are a coffee maker, an electric kettle and cutlery/crockery for cold dishes at your convenience. Drinking water has to be collected at a spring on the river bank or at the well in the farm yard.

It is possible to wash at the tub with cold water, in the lavatory or in the river. In the sauna you can have a hot shower. With respect to toilets you can make your choice: there are an old-fashioned dry toilet and flush toilets as well. The drinking water is pumped up from a well 70 meters under ground and is conditioned for human use. There is a shaft-well in old Estonian style, too.

Basic accommodation

Very basic accommodation can be found in our "kainestusmaja" ("sobering-up cell") in bunk beds for 6 people. Mattress, woolen blankets and a covered pillow in a clean room - you can't think of anything more simple and uncomplicated.

You can even prepare a small meal.

Bed & Breakfast rooms

High quality B&B rooms are on offer on the first floor of the old farm house. Two spacious rooms with double beds and a smaller double room are equipped with minibar, settee and hot & cold water. All rooms are air-conditioned. An ample breakfast buffet will be at your disposal.

You can watch Sat-TV in the sitting room or just browse Estonian, German or English papers or magazines. A shared toilet and a shower are in separate rooms.

Shared toilet and shower are separate.

Holiday home

The holiday home is especially designed for self-catering families with children but is suitable for small groups, too.

A double room, two single rooms, a twin room (bunk beds) and two sofas can be used by up to eight persons. A large, high and bright living room, letting the sun in from morning till evening through spacious windows, with a built-in sitting gallery offers ample space for them, whether in front of the open fire place or at the dining table. The house is heated by radiators and by firewood in a specially designed stove.

A fully equipped kitchenette with refrigerator, microwave oven etc. enables more ambitious dishes.

A veranda to the south-west is intended for sitting in the open on sunny afternoons till late at night.

For your entertainment there are a Sat-TV set, a video player (including a small collection of video films in English and German), various board and card games, shaker and dice, a small library and an audio system. Wireless internet access will keep you connected to the world.

Holiday home and B&B rooms can be used comfortably all year round, even in the coldest winter!

Ground floor: 1 - hall 2 - bathroom 3 - sauna 4 - sitting room 5 - bedroom (bunks) 6 - double room. First floor: 7 - second level veranda 8 - single room 9 - single room. The bathroom has got a shower and a flush toilet. There is a sauna in the house, too. Bed linen is included in the rent; towels and sauna towels will be supplied on request.

Location
Location

The farm "Kalda" in Iigaste/South Estonia

is situated in the border area towards Latvia, 20 km away from the border town of Valga/Valka on the road to Otep and Tartu. Riga and Tallinn are about the same distance away - what should be of interest to clients of Ryan Air and Easyjet. The village of Iigaste is part of Valga community, the nearest hamlet being Laatre. There are a pharmacy and a grocery shop. Iigaste is an agricultural area, surrounded by thick forests. To the west is the town of Valga/Valka, divided between Estonia and Latvia, to the east is the winter sports area around Otepaa, to the south stretches the Karula National Park, a landscape of wooded hills with numerous small lakes. Predominant activities are all about roaming the countryside: in summer by walking, jogging, biking, swimming, canoeing, in winter cross-country skiing, tobogganing, ice-skating and ski-jumping. At any rate there is a lot to discover in fauna and flora all year around. Excursions to the university town of Tartu and to the seaside resort and spa of Parnu make an interesting break.

The farmsted of "Kalda"

Byking and hiking is a good pastime on paths very rarely used by motorized vehicles.

We provide suggestions for just short walks to more challenging hikes, even with overnights. Just ask for advice.

The same applies to more speedy ways of transportation. You can either rent a bike or one of our cars to explore a wider range of the country.

A small beach is 6 km away. Our small river "Vike Emajgi" is not suitable for swimming because of shallow water, but you may rent one of our canoes to start a longer tour on it. Canoe tours can be arranged on the Koiva/Gauja, too, as day tours or with overnights.

The farmsted of "Kalda"

which can be translated as "sloping, near a river bank", is situated in a wide bend of the "Vaike Emajogi" river, that nourishes lake "Worts" and flows on passing through Tartu to lake "Peipsi". It is possible to tour the river by canoe, doing a lot of bathing and swimming on the way. There is a good chance to get some fish on the rod as well. The birds' habitat is a real temptation to bird watchers. Many quite rare species can be watched. - There are beavers in the river, and a lot of deer and small game pay frequent visits to the farm.

Being situated inside the river bend and at the end of the accessing road, farm life is tranquil, quiet and undisturbed.

The farm offers ample space for various groups of guests at a time. Camping sites and accomodation buildings are set up in a way that prevents any interference. Besides the town Valga/Valka and the university city of Tartu there are a variety of sightseeing places in the neighbourhood: both for the historical and cultural interested and the lovers of manor houses and nature. Especially the historians have a wide choice to track down on history: the Danes, Germans, Polish, Swedes, the Russian Tsars, Napoleon, the Sowjets - they all contributed to many accomplishments as well as to lots of harm and suppression to this country and its inhabitants. If you are interested in a certain field of studies - just tell us right away, and we will be delighted to help making your stay as stimulating and prolific as possible. We are prepared to recommend books on history, nature and fiction. There is a small library of Estonian fiction available in German and English translations. Estonia takes pride in being the world champion in offering internet access points. We share this pride by offering wireless internet access on the whole farm area.

FAQ

Is Kalda talu a good place for a family holiday?

Families with children are very welcome. There is ample space for the children to play. Even non-swimmers can bathe in the river safely.

Can we bring our pet?

Yes, you may. We have got dog and a tomcat ourselves.

Who is welcome? And who is not?

Anybody is welcome, no matter, what gender, colour, nationality, religion, sexual orientation you belong to, as long as you are interested in others and tolerant. What we don't like: quarrelling, violence, drugs, discriminating behaviour!

Do you serve lunches and dinners?

Sorry, we don't. But we can recommend some good eating places in the vicinity. Groups can be catered for in cooperation with a nearby catering service.

What about breakfast?

For B&B guests it's included in the price. Other customers can order it, if they don't prefer to use the self catering facilities provided. We serve breakfast as a full Swedish board.

Do you provide simple, cheap accommodation for young people?

Part of our place is designed as a youth hostel. It's our policy to offer inexpensive accommodation for young travellers all year round.

How do you treat the rubbish?

We are eager to promote the idea of separating it, if we just can't avoid it.

Can we have a barbecue?

Yes, you may. We have got a shed full of firewood. There is a campfire site and there are grills of different sizes. Charcoal is for sale at a small fee. Just bring your own meat, sausages and dressings!

What about the quality of drinking water?

It's pumped up from 70 meters under ground and is especially treated for human consumption.

Is it possible to hold conventions, seminars and meetings at Kalda talu?

Our function room can be rented either on a self-catering or on a full service basis. It is suitable for family reunions, weddings, birthday parties, company outings, etc. Banquets can be arranged in cooperation with a nearby catering service. For seminars, conferences or training courses it is equipped with all the technology necessary. There is even WiFi access to the Internet

What is is your special affiliation to scouts and guides?

The owner has been a scout himself in his earlier years and would like to offer chances for younger generations to experience this for themselves. So groups of scouts and guides are very welcome to have their camps, training courses, events ... here.

If you have any more questions, please send an email to puhkekeskus@msn.com

Scouts and guides

Having been a scout in his boyhood and in his prime, the owner of Kalda talu has experienced the values of sharing. There are lots of things you can share with others: your opinions, your ideas, your beliefs, your faith, your abilities, your songs ...

Believe me: when you are sharing something with others, you won't get poorer but richer, for you will get twice as much back. Of course - you have to curious about others, openminded to new experiences, interested in other ways of life ...

That's why I keep supporting young people who join with others, who live together in groups, who are eager to get to know the whole spectrum of life.

Do you feel that way, too? So you are welcome here! Scouts, guides and other members of independent youth organizations can be sure to get any help and support they need.

Canoe tours

We organize day tours or tours with overnights on the rivers Mustjgi and Koiva/Gauja. We'll take you and the canoes to the starting point. If you want a tour of more than one day, we'll equip you with a tent and sleeping bags. On the banks of Koiva/Gauja there are several resting places. At the end of the tour we will pick you up again.

Similar conditions apply to tours on Vike-Emajgi. You will start off at our place and can paddle down the river till Tartu if you feel strong enough ... There we will pick you up again.

We have to adjust the detailed plan of the tour together with you in order to avoid any standard proceedures.

Stalking and hunting tours

We arrange the contact to a local hunter by previous enquiery. You can discuss all necessary details with him.
